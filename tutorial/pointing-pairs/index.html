Pointing Pairs (Locked Candidates Type 1)

1. What You Will Master

In this section, you will learn the concept of Pointing Pairs. You will discover how a constraint inside a 3×3 Box can impact the entire Row or Column that passes through it. You will master the skill of seeing candidates that "point" like a laser, eliminating possibilities far across the grid.

2. Main Content

The Concept

A Pointing Pair (or Pointing Triple) occurs when a specific candidate appears two or three times within a Box, but all those instances sit in the same Row (or Column).

Because the number must exist somewhere in that Box, and its only available spots are in that specific Row, we can conclude that the number cannot exist anywhere else in that Row outside of the Box.

The Logic Step-by-Step

Let's look at Box 1 (top-left).

Scan: You are looking for the number '4'.

Observation: In Box 1, the number '4' is not placed yet. However, looking at your pencil marks, you see that the '4' can only go in R1C2 or R1C3.

Alignment: Notice that both R1C2 and R1C3 are in Row 1.

Deduction:

The '4' of Box 1 must be in Row 1.

Therefore, Row 1 is "occupied" by Box 1 regarding the number '4'.

Action: You can look to the right (Box 2 and Box 3) along Row 1 and eliminate any pencil marks for '4'.

The "Pointing" Metaphor

Think of the pair as an arrow or a gun.

The candidates are aligned in a box.

They "point" outwards along the line.

Any candidate in the line of fire gets eliminated.

Why It Matters

Pointing Pairs are often the key to breaking a deadlock. A Pointing Pair in Box 1 might eliminate a candidate in Box 3, which in turn reveals a Naked Single. It is a form of "action at a distance."

3. Section Summary

Pointing Pairs relies on the rule: "If a candidate is locked to a single line within a box, it is removed from the rest of that line." This is the first intersection technique you should look for. Whenever you use Snyder Notation and mark two spots in a box, check immediately: Are they aligned? If yes, look down the line and see if they shoot anything.
